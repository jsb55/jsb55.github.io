Lionel Messi

Lionel Andrés Messi Cuccittini is an Argentine soccer player who plays as a forward for Barcelona FC. Often regarded as one of the best in the world and of all time, Messi is a force to be reckoned with. With his small stature and low center of gravity, Messi is able to dribble and move with incredible precision.

Accolades

  • 10 League titles
  • 4 UEFA Champions League titles
  • 5 Ballon d'Or awards
  • ~700 goals for club and country
