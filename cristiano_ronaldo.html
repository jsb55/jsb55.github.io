Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro is a Portuguese soccer player who currently plays for the Italian club Juventus. He is widely regarded as one of the best players in the world as well as one of the best of all time. His goal scoring ability is unparalleled and strikes fear into opponents hearts when he enters the pitch.

Accolades

  • 6 League titles
  • 5 UEFA Champions League titles
  • 5 Ballon d'Or awards
  • >700 goals for club and country
